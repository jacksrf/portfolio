About Me

DEVELOP

I am a full-stack developer with a focus on Ruby and Javascript and a concentration on NodeJS and Rails. My other development skills include SQL, BackboneJS, Sinatra and ExpressJS.

DESIGN

As a designer, I have learned to mesh my development background with the desire to bring an engaging experience to the user. Using frameworks such as Twitter Boostrap and advanced skills in HTML5 and CSS3, I strive to create a smooth and vivid design.

INNOVATE

From personal experience ranging from start-ups, (most recently Freetextbooks.com - Techstars Austin Summer 2014, which disrupted the college textbook industry) to Corporations; I have learned to innovate and adapt projects to the ever changing landscape that is the tech world.

Chatter is a simple NodeJs Websockets app that is also mobile responsive. It consists of a server and client Javascript file. Some of the features involved are custom usernames and avatars, posting of images, gifs and vines, private messaging and an online users list.
